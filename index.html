AS Wealth & Training Pvt. Ltd Admission & Certificate Process.

Admission Process

1. WhatsApp to Mentor: You will WhatsApp your name and the course you enrolled in to the mentor for processing your admission.

2. Admission Approval: After your admission is approved, you can stay in regular touch with your mentor on WhatsApp and call during working hours (with an appointment).

3. Dedicated Relation Manager: You will be assigned a dedicated relation manager to help you with your learning . You can call or WhatsApp during working hours.

4. Live Classes/Webinars: You will attend live Zoom classes or webinars if applicable for your course. The course page mentions if there are classes for your program and its schedule. Attendance is mandatory. For some subjects, AS Wealth & Training Pvt. Ltd is the only organization that provides live Zoom webinars by experts in different fields to its students.

5. Study Plan Discussion: The instructor will discuss a study plan with you.

6. Self-Study Commitment:You will do self-study of 2 to 4 hours per week in addition to classes.

7. Feedback to Mentor: You will provide updates and feedback about classes to your mentor regularly on WhatsApp during working hours.

8. Instructor and Mentor Roles: : Your instructor is for the study plan, and your mentor is for career guidance.

9. Certificate Dispatch: Upon completing the course and demonstrating your stock market practice, a hard copy certificate from AS Wealth & Training Pvt. Ltd. will be couriered to you within two months. There are no hidden charges.

10. Lifetime Validity: The certificate will mention that it is valid for a lifetime. No renewal fee.

11. Payment: You pay the fee. Your transaction is completely secured.

12. Inclusive Fee: The fee includes everything (registration fee, instructor support, certificate fee, etc.).

13. No Hidden Charges: Payment: There are no hidden charges.

14. Payment Receipt: You will receive a payment receipt via email immediately after payment. Confirmation of your seat will be emailed after verifying your eligibility.

15. Relation Manager Contact: A relation manager will contact you within a few hours after payment and will provide you with the WhatsApp and calling number of your mentor.
